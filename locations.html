    The English Book Exchange

    Address: Kloveniersburgwal 56

    The English Book Exchange is located close to the centrum of Amsterdam. It is a 17 minute walk from Amsterdam Centraal and is also partly accessible by public transport. The bookstore is a walking distance away from the Zeedijk, which is considered the China town from Amsterdam.
